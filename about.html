The story

Built for people who were handed a report and left to guess.

Too many people walk out of a lab with a printout full of acronyms and arrows, and no idea what it means for them. They Google in a panic, or do nothing until it's serious.

PulsingLabs closes that gap: book in minutes, get every result explained in plain English (or chat with the AI in Urdu), see exactly where you stand against age- and sex-aware ranges, and track how you trend over time, for you and your whole family.

What we stand for

Our principles

Clinically grounded

Reference ranges follow evidence-based guidelines (ADA, AASLD, WHO, NICE and more), tuned to age, sex and pregnancy, verified by a cross-engine test suite.

Privacy by default

Your results are yours. Encrypted in transit, never sold, and for companies only ever shown as anonymised aggregates.

Built for everyone

The AI assistant chats in Urdu or English, understanding your results shouldn't depend on which language you're comfortable in.

Clarity over jargon

Every explanation is written to be understood, calm, plain, and actionable, never alarming.

Whole-family care

One account covers parents, kids and spouse, so a family's health lives in one place.

Safety first

The AI assistant escalates urgent symptoms to emergency guidance and never gives unsafe dosing advice.
