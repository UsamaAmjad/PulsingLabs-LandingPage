Privacy Policy

Your health data is sensitive. Here's exactly how we handle it.

Last updated: 4 July 2026

1. Who we are

PulsingLabs ("we", "us") is a digital health platform that enables you to book blood test appointments, view lab results with clinical insights, and access AI-powered health guidance. We are committed to protecting your personal and health data. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

2. Data we collect

  • Identity data: full name, date of birth and biological sex (used to personalise your reference ranges).
  • Contact data: email address and phone number.
  • Health data: blood test results, appointment history, and lab reports you import.
  • Family profiles: data you add for family members you manage.
  • Usage data: session activity and your AI chat history (last 30 messages).
  • Device data: push notification subscription tokens.

We collect only what is necessary to deliver the service.

3. How we use your data

  • To book and manage lab appointments on your behalf and deliver your results.
  • To display your results with clinical reference ranges, trends and plain-English explanations.
  • To generate AI health insights (see "Third parties" below).
  • To send transactional emails (booking confirmations, result notifications) and push notifications you have opted into.
  • To show your employer, if you join a company programme, anonymised team wellness trends only.

4. Legal basis for processing

  • Your explicit consent, given at registration and for optional features.
  • Performance of a contract, providing the service you booked.
  • Legitimate interests, platform security and fraud prevention.

5. Third parties

  • Email delivery provider: transactional emails are sent through our email provider, subject to its privacy policy.
  • Google Gemini AI: powers AI health insights. Prompts contain anonymised data only (never your name or email), subject to Google's AI data usage policy.
  • Your employer (company admin): if you join a company programme, your admin can see your appointment status and whether results have been received. They cannot see individual test values unless you share them.

We do not sell your data to any third party.

6. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data and health records are permanently erased within 30 days. AI chat sessions are limited to your last 30 messages and are not used to train AI models.

7. Your privacy at work

If your account is part of a corporate plan, your employer only ever sees anonymised, aggregate statistics. Your individual test values and personal health records are never visible to your employer.

8. Your rights (GDPR)

  • Access & export: download your full account and results at any time.
  • Correction: correct inaccurate data from your profile.
  • Deletion: delete your account and all associated data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: take your data with you in a standard format.
  • Withdrawal: withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, use the account options in your profile or contact us at contact@pulsinglabs.com.

9. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. Authentication uses short-lived tokens, and access to health data is restricted to the data owner and authorised company admins.
