Questions

Pricing FAQ

Is the app really free for individuals?

Yes. Downloading and using PulsingLabs is free, you only pay the lab for the test packages you choose to book. There's no subscription for personal use.

How does the corporate trial work?

Corporate accounts get 30 days of full access at no charge, no credit card required. After the trial, billing is PKR 1,500 per active employee per month. Cancel anytime.

What counts as an "active employee"?

Any approved employee account that logged at least one activity, an appointment booked or a result viewed, in the billing month.

Is my health data private from my employer?

Always. Corporate dashboards show only anonymised, aggregate statistics. Employers never see individual test values or personal health records.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings and access continues to the end of the current billing period. No lock-in.

Do you offer non-profit or academic discounts?

Yes, email contact@pulsinglabs.com and we'll sort out special pricing.
